About Reflexa

Reflexa is your comprehensive digital wellness platform designed specifically for students. Here's what we offer:

  • Personal Journal: Track your daily emotions and thoughts in a private, secure space.
  • Mood Tracking: Monitor your emotional well-being with our intuitive mood tracking system.
  • Mental Health Resources: Access expert-curated articles, videos, and podcasts.
  • Live Workshops: Join interactive sessions on stress management, mindfulness, and self-care.
  • Community Support: Connect with peers in a safe, supportive environment.

Start your journey to better mental health today!
